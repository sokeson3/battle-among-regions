Each region commands unique armies, powerful spells, cunning traps, and legendary landmarks. As a general, you must build your deck, outwit your opponents, and lead your homeland to victory.

The Four Regions

Defensive & Resilient

Ice and frost warriors who endure all. The Northern region excels at building impenetrable defenses, healing, and outlasting opponents with unbreakable fortitude.

High DEF Healing Fortify

Eastern

Cunning & Spell-Focused

Masters of stealth and ancient scrolls. The Eastern region dominates through spell synergies, shadow mechanics, card advantage, and devastating traps.
